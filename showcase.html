My Experience

Red Dead Redemption 2 is one of the best games I have ever played. What impressed me the most is how realistic and detailed the world feels.

I really enjoyed exploring the map, riding through the landscapes, and discovering small details everywhere. The story is very emotional and different from most other games.

Arthur Morgan is one of my favorite characters because he changes a lot during the game. His development makes the story much more interesting.
